About our Dental Clinic

Welcome to Bright Smile clinic, your trusted partner in oral health. Located at 200 Garrity Crescent in the heart of Ottawa, we are dedicated to providing you with the highest quality dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Our Mission

At Bright Smile Clinic, our mission is to enhance the dental health and well-being of our community by providing exceptional, personalized care in a comfortable and modern environment. We believe everyone deserves access to affordable dental care, which is why we offer competitive prices without compromising on quality.

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services, from routine cleanings and exams to advanced restorative treatments. Our state-of-the-art clinic is equipped with the latest dental technologies to provide efficient and effective treatments with your comfort in mind.

Community Involvement

Bright Smile Clinic is a proud member of the Ottawa community. We actively participate in various community outreach programs, dental health education initiatives, and free dental clinics for underserved populations. Our team regularly conducts workshops at local schools and participates in health fairs to promote oral health awareness.
